Rachel McGarry ǀ Window into the Artist’s Mind: Master Drawings from the MIA

Last occurred: Thursday, July 24 at 6:30 pm

George Romney, Study for the “Lapland Witch,” c.1775-77, The John R. Van Derlip Fund 68.51

SOLD OUT – (Limited Free Overflow Seating Available in Wells Fargo Room)

Rachel McGarry, curator of “Marks of Genius,” offers a rare glimpse of treasures usually hidden from view: works on paper, sensitive to light exposure, that are exhibited only briefly. This is your chance to hear their stories brought to light, illuminating extraordinary drawings by Guercino, George Romney, Jean-François Millet, Edgar Degas, Käthe Kollwitz, Egon Schiele, Piet Mondrian, and Roy Lichtenstein, and other masters who deserve to be better known.

$10; $5 members; free for members of the Prints and Drawings Affinity Group.
